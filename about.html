About Us

My name is Nikhil Kumar, I am 25 years old and come from Bavaria, more precisely from the wonderful Altmühltal Nature Park. The love for photography began in 2014 when I spontaneously bought my first SLR camera. I found the first attempts to photograph insects and other small animals, to photograph the flowers in the garden or to capture water droplets in flight extremely exciting. Until one day the old, empty BayWa site behind our house piqued my interest. I entered the huge warehouse with my camera through one of the high iron gates. Time seemed to stand still here. The sparse light that came through blind windows that were missing a piece of glass here and there, the floor covered with a thick film of oil and dust, it was fascinating for me and I tried to capture this mood with my camera. The silence that such a place brings with it is pure tension. Life goes on in front of the gates, the noise of engines, people running around hectically but inside you often only hear the shutter release of the camera echoing through the endless corridors.
